UNC Pleiades

About us...

UNC Pleiades is the Club Ultimate Frisbee team at the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill competing in the USA Ultimate D1 Women's College Division. Pleiades was first founded in 1995, named after the Pleaides star cluster also called "The Seven Sisters." With seven athletes competing on the field at a time, this symbolic name represents the bond between players both on and off the field. Pleiades is a team and a tight-knit community that values competition, dedication, and friendship.

Pleiades first qualified for nationals in 2002, returning in 2017 and qualifying every year since. Pleiades has won the last 3 College Championships, making them back-to-back-to-back National Champions. The group of driven athletes making up this year's current roster have set big goals and have their sights on another championship. This webpage provides a place for fans, friends, and family to keep up to date with the Pleiades and for those interested to learn more about the team both past and present.
