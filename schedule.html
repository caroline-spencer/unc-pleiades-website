2023-2024 Tournament Schedule

The college ultimate season takes place over the course of the academic year, beginning in the fall and ending in the spring. UNC Pleiades generally treats the Fall season as a period of growth and development for the team where the team attends fewer and less competitive tournaments. The competitive season is in the spring which culminates with the USAU D1 College Championship Series. Pleiades competes in North Carolina Conferences, Atlantic Coast Regionals, and then Nationals. Only select teams move on from Sectionals to Regionals, and even fewer from Regionals to Nationals, where 20 teams from across the country compete for the National Championship in each division.
