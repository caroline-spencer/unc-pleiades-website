UNC PLEIADES About Schedule Rosters

Team Rosters

This page contains lists of players and coaches that were a part of UNC Pleiades each year. First year players, or rookies, are selected through a tryout process. Captains are elected by the team at the end of the previous season and serve as leaders for the team. Pleiades is grateful to have talented and knowledgeable coaches that volunteer their time to lead and develop the team. Pleiades has won the last 3 and 3 total College National Championship titles, winning the first title in program history in 2021.This page credits each roster that won a Championship.

Key:

* denotes rookies

** denotes captains

(C) denotes coaches

Note: Spring 2020 and Spring 2021 seasons were cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The 2021 Series was postponed to Fall 2021, with the national championships occuring in Decemeber 2021. 2020 and 2021 graduates were allowed to participate in the Fall 2021 Series.
